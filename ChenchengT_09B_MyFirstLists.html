• Sustainable Architectures Example

List of sustainable materials:

  1. Hempcrete

    • Biodegradable, carbon-negative, insulating, and mold-resistant.
    • Can be used for: Wall infill, insulation.

  2. Rammed Earth

    • Made from natural soil compacted into forms; provides excellent thermal mass, minimizing heating/cooling needs.
    • Can be used for: Walls and foundations.

  3. Recycled Steel

    • Reduces the need for mining, is highly durable, and recyclable.
    • Can be used for: Structural framework, roofing, and reinforcement.

  4. Low-E Glass

    • Reduces heating and cooling costs by minimizing heat transfer
    • Can be used for: Windows and facades.
