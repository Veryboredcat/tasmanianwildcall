As a very distant relative of the Kangaroo, Tasmanian Devils belong to the family Dasyuridae within the order Dasyuromorphia. Like all dasyurids, the devil has 14 chromosomes.
The devils are marsupials closely related to quolls and the extinct Thylacine (Tasmanian Tiger).

When did the devils make its appearance in geologic time?

Time Period (Million Years Ago) Event / Information

~14 The dasyurid family radiated into many new forms.
